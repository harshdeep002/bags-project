THE KEY TO OUR DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Every bag in the Carlton Edge series is designed like a masterpiece. And just like how every masterpiece originates from a particular idea, thought or value, our bags resonate two philosophies: unique style and effortless travel.

UNIQUE STYLE

A unique look is one that will always stand out in a crowd. It is a statement that will always make the headlines in any era. To create such a look for the range, our designers drew inspiration from vintage as well as contemporary design philosophies. The beauty of which is expressed in the refined colour palettes and clean contours. Our bags are also an exhibition of elaborate detailing, glimpses of which you will find in the textured looks, handcrafted stitching and even in the choice of hardware.

EFFORTLESS TRAVEL

At Carlton, our design moves in sync with current trends and frequent fliers' specific needs, because our objective is to create bags whose beauty is matched by their utility. So for the Carlton Edge bags, our designers have used innovation to translate these consumer insights and design cues into chic, convenient features. Like the Neo-pack, the uniquely designed convi-pack or even the suitor kit, that take away the stress from travelling. Every bag in the Carlton Edge series is thus your license to indulge in effortless travel with absolute style.
